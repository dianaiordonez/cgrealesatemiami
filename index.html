Our Favorite Areas

Coral Gables

Coral Gables, Miami, is a vibrant and upscale city known for its elegant Mediterranean Revival architecture, lush greenery, and affluent neighborhoods, offering a blend of cultural richness and tropical charm.

Coconut Grove

Coconut Grove, Miami, exudes a bohemian flair with its eclectic mix of art galleries, outdoor cafes, and lush parks, creating a laid-back yet vibrant atmosphere distinct from the elegance of Coral Gables.

South Miami

South Miami is a charming suburban enclave within the Miami metropolitan area, characterized by its family-friendly neighborhoods, tree-lined streets, and diverse community atmosphere, offering a relaxed residential setting with easy access to urban amenities and cultural attractions.
